About

Building foundations that last

John Moss is a fourth-generation land steward, entrepreneur, and technology builder based in Wisconsin Rapids. At 40, he runs multiple ventures that bridge the gap between agricultural tradition and modern technology.

From a 150-acre family farm with over a century of history to founding Elm Lake Labs and Blossom, John's work is guided by a single principle: create systems that reduce friction, build leverage, and leave people more capable.

"To build things that last — foundations that turn chaos into clarity through systems that reduce friction, create leverage, and leave people more capable than when I found them."
