about

Where tradition meets deliberate innovation.

In Wisconsin Rapids, John Moss cultivates both land and technology. A fourth-generation cranberry farmer and entrepreneur, he builds companies at the intersection of agriculture and modern systems — tools that reduce complexity and create lasting value.

His approach mirrors wabi-sabi: finding beauty in the imperfect, valuing substance over spectacle, building with patience and intention. The 150-acre family farm is not just heritage — it's the foundation from which everything grows.
