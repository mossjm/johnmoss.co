About

John Moss is the fourth-generation steward of Elm Lake Cranberry, a 150-acre, century-old family cranberry operation in Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin. Under his leadership, the farm has become one of the most technologically advanced operations in the cranberry industry.

Where others see an old family farm, John sees a platform for innovation — integrating precision irrigation, environmental sensing, drone mapping, and autonomous equipment into every layer of the operation.

Through Elm Lake Labs, his agricultural technology venture, John is extending those same tools beyond his own operation. The Blossom SaaS platform, GIS/drone services, and GPS/GNSS solutions are bringing modern precision agriculture to growers across the industry.

As President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board and incoming Ocean Spray Board member, he works at the intersection of technology adoption and industry policy — building infrastructure that compounds.
