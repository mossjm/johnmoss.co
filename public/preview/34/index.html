About

A quiet determination
to build what endures.

Based in Wisconsin Rapids, John Moss bridges the divide between heritage and innovation. A fourth-generation cranberry farmer turned technology entrepreneur, his work is defined by a commitment to building lasting infrastructure — systems that create leverage and leave people more capable than before.

At 40, he runs multiple ventures while stewarding 150 acres of family land that has been in continuous cultivation for over a century. The farm is both foundation and proving ground.
