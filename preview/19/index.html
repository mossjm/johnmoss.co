Heritage Meets Innovation

John Moss has transformed Elm Lake Cranberry — a century-old family operation — into one of the most technologically advanced cranberry farms in the industry.

Precision irrigation, environmental sensing, drone mapping, and autonomous equipment now work alongside the timeless principles of stewardship passed down through four generations.

Through Elm Lake Labs, he's building the infrastructure for an entire industry's technological transformation.
