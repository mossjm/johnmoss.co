About

Where heritage meets the leading edge of technology.

I'm an entrepreneur based in central Wisconsin, running multiple ventures across artificial intelligence, enterprise software, and agriculture. I see patterns where others see chaos, and I build systems that turn complexity into leverage.

My family has worked the same land for over 100 years — four generations building something that lasts. That perspective shapes everything I do: build for durability, not just speed. Invest in systems that compound. Leave things better than you found them.

Today, I apply that philosophy across technology, agriculture, and industry leadership — always looking for where deep domain expertise and cutting-edge technology create outsized value.

"Build things that last — foundations that turn chaos into clarity through systems that reduce friction, create leverage, and leave people more capable."

— Personal Philosophy
