Section 02 — About

John Moss, 40, is the tech-forward cranberry grower. He's taken a century-old family operation and woven technology into every layer — from precision irrigation systems that respond to real-time environmental data to drone-mapped field surveys.

Through Elm Lake Labs, he's extending those tools beyond his own operation, building the infrastructure for an entire industry's technological transformation.

As President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board and incoming Ocean Spray board member, he works at the intersection of technology adoption and industry policy.
