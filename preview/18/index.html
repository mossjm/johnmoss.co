About

Building at the intersection of heritage and innovation.

John Moss is the fourth-generation owner and general manager of Elm Lake Cranberry, a 150-acre, century-old family cranberry operation in Wisconsin Rapids. Under his stewardship, the farm has become one of the most technologically advanced in the industry.

Through Elm Lake Labs, his agricultural technology venture, John is extending precision agriculture tools — from SaaS platforms to drone mapping to autonomous equipment — to growers across the cranberry industry.

As President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board and incoming member of the Ocean Spray Board of Directors, he works at the intersection of technology adoption and industry governance.
