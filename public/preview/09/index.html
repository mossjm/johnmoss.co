About

At Elm Lake Cranberry, I've transformed a century-old family operation into one of the most technologically advanced in the industry. Agricultural leader and innovator known industry-wide for bridging tradition and technology.

Through Elm Lake Labs, I'm bringing that same innovation to growers across the sector. As President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board and incoming member of the Ocean Spray Board of Directors, I work at the intersection of technology adoption and industry policy.
