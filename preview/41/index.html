Room II · The Land

Four generations of

The Elm Lake Cranberry farm — 150 acres of bogs in Wisconsin Rapids — has been in the Moss family for over a century. What began as a family operation has evolved into a living laboratory where tradition meets precision agriculture, and the wisdom of generations informs the application of cutting-edge technology.
