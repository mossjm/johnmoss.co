About

Heritage Meets Innovation

John Moss is the fourth-generation steward of Elm Lake Cranberry, a 150-acre, century-old family cranberry operation in central Wisconsin.

He's transformed a traditional operation into the industry's most technologically advanced, integrating precision irrigation, environmental sensing, drone mapping, and autonomous equipment.

Through Elm Lake Labs, he's building the tools for an entire industry's technological transformation.
