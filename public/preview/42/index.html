About

Where heritage meets
thoughtful innovation.

Based in Wisconsin Rapids, John Moss is a fourth-generation cranberry farmer turned technology entrepreneur. He builds companies at the intersection of agriculture and modern systems — creating tools that reduce complexity and leave lasting value. At 40, he manages a 150-acre family farm while leading multiple ventures.
