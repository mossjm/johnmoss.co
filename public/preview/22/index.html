About

Building foundations that outlast the builder

John Moss is a fourth-generation land steward, entrepreneur, and technology builder based in Wisconsin Rapids. At 40, he leads multiple ventures spanning agriculture, SaaS, and equipment — each built on the same core principle.

From a 150-acre family farm with over a century of history to founding Elm Lake Labs and Blossom, John's work is driven by one goal: build systems that reduce friction, create leverage, and leave people more capable.

"To build things that last — foundations that turn chaos into clarity through systems that reduce friction, create leverage, and leave people more capable than when I found them."
