The Journey

From century-old marshland to the cutting edge of ag-tech

Early 1900s

A Family Legacy Begins

The Moss family establishes Elm Lake Cranberry in Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin. What begins as a modest cranberry operation will grow into a 150-acre farm that spans generations.

Early cranberry harvesting by hand — a line of workers raking the marshes as the Moss family did for decades.

Generation 1

Joe & Rose Moss

The first generation to steward the land. Joe and Rose established the principles that would guide the family for over a century.

Joe and Rose Moss — the founders of the Elm Lake Cranberry legacy.

Generations 2 & 3

Building the Foundation

Each generation adds to the legacy — expanding acreage, improving water management, and establishing Elm Lake as a cornerstone of the Wisconsin cranberry industry.

Mike and Diane Moss — John's parents, the third generation to steward the operation.

The Fourth Generation

John Takes the Helm

John Moss becomes the fourth-generation steward of Elm Lake Cranberry. With a vision that honors tradition while embracing technology, he begins transforming the operation into one of the most advanced in the industry.

John & Melissa Moss

The fourth generation — John and his wife Melissa at the family marshes.

Agriculture Precision Farming
The Innovation Leap

Founding Elm Lake Labs

Recognizing that the tools he built for his own farm could transform the entire industry, John founds Elm Lake Labs — an agricultural technology company bringing precision tools to cranberry growers nationwide.

Elm Lake Labs

GIS/drone services, GPS/GNSS solutions, FJ Dynamics dealer, and the Blossom SaaS platform.

Ag-Tech SaaS Drones
Building the Platform

Blossom SaaS Launch

The Blossom platform launches — a SaaS farm management system purpose-built for cranberry operations. Field tracking, crew management, and operational intelligence in one unified system.

Blossom

Farm management software designed from the ground up for cranberry growers.

SaaS Farm Management
Industry Leadership

President, Wisconsin Cranberry Board

John is elected President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board, bringing his technology-forward perspective to industry policy, research priorities, and statewide cranberry advocacy.

2026

Ocean Spray Board of Directors

John joins the Board of Directors of Ocean Spray, representing grower interests at the cooperative level and shaping the strategic direction of America's premier cranberry brand.
