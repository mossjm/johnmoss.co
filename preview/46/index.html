About

At forty, John Moss bridges two worlds — the deeply rooted traditions of a family cranberry farm spanning four generations and the cutting edge of agricultural technology. Based in Wisconsin Rapids, he builds companies that create lasting infrastructure: tools and systems that empower others to do more with less. His 150-acre farm is both foundation and proving ground.
