About

A quiet determination
to build what endures.

Based in Wisconsin Rapids, John Moss bridges four generations of cranberry farming with cutting-edge technology. At 40, he manages a 150-acre family farm while building companies that create lasting infrastructure — tools and systems that empower others and reduce the complexity of modern agriculture.
