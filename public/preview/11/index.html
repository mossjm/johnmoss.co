The Story

Heritage Meets Innovation

Fourth-generation steward of Elm Lake Cranberry, John Moss has transformed a century-old family operation spanning 150 acres into one of the most technologically advanced cranberry farms in the industry.

Where others see tradition and technology as opposing forces, John sees a continuum — each generation building on the last, adding new tools without abandoning timeless principles.

"To build things that last — foundations that turn chaos into clarity."

Through Elm Lake Labs, he's extending that vision beyond his own operation, bringing precision agriculture tools to growers across the industry.
