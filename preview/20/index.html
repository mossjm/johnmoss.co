About

The tech-forward
cranberry grower

John Moss, 40, is the fourth-generation steward of Elm Lake Cranberry — a 150-acre, century-old family cranberry operation in Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin.

Under his leadership, the farm has become one of the most technologically advanced in the cranberry industry, integrating precision irrigation, environmental sensing, drone mapping, and autonomous equipment.

Through Elm Lake Labs, his agricultural technology venture, John is extending those same tools beyond his own operation — building the infrastructure for an entire industry's technological transformation.

As President of the Wisconsin Cranberry Board and incoming Ocean Spray Board member, he works at the intersection of technology adoption and industry governance, building systems that compound.
